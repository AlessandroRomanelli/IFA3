some history

What are we talking about?

Iron Front is a tactical shooter videogame set in the Eastern Front of World War 2, built by AWAR - a group of former OFP and senior ArmA modders.
It was released in middle 2012 as a standalone game using the Real Virtuality 3 engine, the same one that in the same time was powering ArmA2: Operation Arrowhead.

Always one step ahead

What was so special about it?

  • Five terrains of different sizes but a single costant: richness of detail
  • A large variety of very high quality and historically accurate units, arsenal and vehicles
  • A German and Russian singleplayer campaign across eight missions in an authentic and life-like setting
  • Advanced gameplay features, such as the high fidelty tank simulation, gun towing, the artillery system or the realistic mine system
  • Immersive, dynamic, variable, scalable gameplay with gear ranging from early to the late war era equipment
