Iron Front

a ww2 total conversion mod for ArmA 3

some history

What are we talking about?

Iron Front is a tactical shooter videogame set in the Eastern Front of World War 2, built by AWAR - a group of former OFP and senior ArmA modders.
It was released in middle 2012 as a standalone game using the Real Virtuality 3 engine, the same one that in the same time was powering ArmA2: Operation Arrowhead.

Always one step ahead

What is so special about it?

  • Five terrains of different sizes with a single costant: richness of detail
  • Large variety of high quality and historically accurate units, arsenal and vehicles
  • German and Russian singleplayer campaigns across eight missions in an authentic and life-like setting
  • Advanced gameplay features, such as the high fidelty tank simulation, gun towing, the artillery system or the realistic mine system
  • Immersive, dynamic, variable, scalable gameplay with gear ranging from early to the late war era equipment

Leading evolution

Where are we going?

Our development team has ported the original game assets to ArmA3 for it to run on the latest RV engine by Bohemia Interactive Studios.
This most recent, ported version of Iron Front, called IFA3, takes advantage and merges many of the new technologies and features of ArmA3 and it's continously developed to provide a fully supported, enjoyable, bug-free version of Iron Front.
